Favorite Recipe - Penne alla Vodka

Penne alla Vodka is a very popular dish made with the following ingredients.

  • 16oz Penne Pasta
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • One finely chopped medium onion
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup of vodka
  • One 14-ounce can of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan, for serving
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package
  2. Saute onions and garlic in 2 tablespoons of butter and olive oil over medium heat until fragrant and soft.
  3. Remove pan from heat and pour in the one cup of vodka
  4. Place pan back over heat and reduce for 3 minutes before pouring in the tomato sauce.
  5. Stir the mixture untiil combined then turn heat to low.
  6. Add in the heavy cream and stir to combine before turning heat to the lowest setting.
  7. Add red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to taste before stirring in remaining tablespoon of butter.
  8. Add in drained pasta and stir to combine.
  9. Serve with sprinked Parmesan and more red pepper flakes if desired.
  10. Enjoy!
